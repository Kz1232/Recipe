Pineapple Juice with mint

Ingredients

  • 2 cup pineapple
  • 1 tablespoon black salt
  • ice cubes as required
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 mint leaves
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 2 tablespoon sugar

How to make a Pineapple Juice?🤔

Step 1: Cut pineapple in pieces

Peel the skin of the pineapple and cut into cubes. Keep aside.

Step 2: Add all the spices with sugar

Add sugar, black pepper powder, roasted cumin powder and black salt. Blend well and strain it and transfer to a glass.

Step 3: Garnish and serve chilled

Garnish with few mint leaves. Add ice cubes and now you are ready to serve pineapple juice.
